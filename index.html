Welcome to my Homepage

Since May 2019, I work at Surrey Space Center as a Post Doctoral Research Associate on motion planning and control of robot arms in space. I am a member of Future AI and Robotics for Space ( FAIR-SPACE ) Hub. Currently, I am the lead developer of the simulator named as Spacecraft Manipulator Arm Simulation Hybrid engine ( SM@SHe ) which is capable of autonomous trajectory learning and control of free-floating spacecraft arms for space debris removal, on-orbit servicing and rendezvous & docking. The project is on-going and future versions of SM@SHe will have enhanced support for several arms on the same spacecraft and multi-DOF robot arms and ROS

Prior to the above, I worked as a Post Doctoral Research Associate at the Lincoln Centre for Autonomous Systems ( LCAS ), School of Computer Science, University of Lincoln, UK, headed by Prof. Gerhard Neumann. The main focus of my work was on robot control, manipulation, motion planning by learning from demonstrations and end-effector design. Check out my IROS 2019 paper here and the video.

I have carried out my doctoral thesis work in Robotics and Design Lab, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore under the guidance of Prof.Ashitava Ghosal.
